M S Dhoni

MS Dhoni is a legendary Indian cricketer, known for his exceptional leadership as captain, powerful right-handed batting, and skilled wicketkeeping. Born in Ranchi, Jharkhand, on July 7, 1981, he is one of the greatest finishers in limited-overs cricket and famously led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup and the 2011 Cricket World Cup.
